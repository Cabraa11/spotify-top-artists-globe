## Why this project exists
- **Curiosity:** See where the most talented / top artists (Spotify Top 100) were **born** – where they’re from, on a map.
- That curiosity is fulfilled; the map does that.

## Why this doc exists
- You want to **train marketing, advertising, branding, and creative skills** – you see that as more important than software for scaling (you built this with “pure English,” no code).
- You believe a lot of people would be interested in this on X and elsewhere – but **getting them actually interested is the whole marketing challenge**. This doc is so you don’t forget and can execute later.

## The challenge
- Making people care and click: even if the project is interesting, you still have to **market it** so they see it and want to look.

## Plan: where to market
- **X (Twitter)** – posts, threads, or short clips pointing to the map.
- **TikTok** – short-form; show the map, “where are the top artists from?”, CTA to try it.
- **Instagram** – **Reels** (short-form); same idea, adapted to Reels format.
- **YouTube** – **Shorts**; same idea, adapted to Shorts.

Each platform is different; the challenge is to **adapt the hook and format** per channel while keeping the same core message: “See where the Top 100 artists are from – on a globe.”
